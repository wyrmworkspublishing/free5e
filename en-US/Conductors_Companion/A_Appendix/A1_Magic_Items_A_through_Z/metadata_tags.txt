The following tags are supported by the item_extractor python script:

[_metadata_:item_name]:- "Item Name"
[_metadata_:item_original_name]:- "Item Name in the SRD 5.1"
[_metadata_:size]:- "Large"
[_metadata_:item_type]:- "Item Type"
[_metadata_:armor_type]:- "Armor Type"
[_metadata_:armor_modification]:- "true"
[_metadata_:ac_modifier]:- "AC Modifier"
[_metadata_:ammunition_type]:- "Ammunition Type"
[_metadata_:ammunition_modification]:- "false"
[_metadata_:weapon_damage_modifier]:- "Weapon Damage Modifier"
[_metadata_:item_is_worn]:- "false"
[_metadata_:item_is_worn_body_part]:- "Part of Body, on which the item is worn"
[_metadata_:item_rarity]:- "Item Rarity"
[_metadata_:item_cursed]:- "false"
[_metadata_:modifies_stat]:- "false"
[_metadata_:modified_stat]:- "Modified Stat"
[_metadata_:modifies_check]:- "Modified Check"
[_metadata_:modifies_check_condition]:- "Condition under which a check is modified"
[_metadata_:check_modification]:- "How the check is modified"
[_metadata_:modified_stat_formula]:- "min(19,current)"
[_metadata_:modifies_save]:- "false"
[_metadata_:modified_save]:- "Modified Save"
[_metadata_:save_modification]:- "Save Modifications"
[_metadata_:requires_attunement]:- "false"
[_metadata_:requires_attunement_by_alignment]:- "any"
[_metadata_:requires_attunement_by_ancestry]:- "any"
[_metadata_:requires_attunement_by_class]:- "any"
[_metadata_:requires_attunement_by_spellcaster]:- "false"
[_metadata_:adds_action]:- "false"
[_metadata_:adds_bonus_action]:- "false"
[_metadata_:adds_reaction]:- "false"
[_metadata_:required_check_type]:- "Stat or Attribute"
[_metadata_:required_check_dc]:- "Check DC"
[_metadata_:casts_spells]:- "Spell Names (separated by semicolon)"
[_metadata_:added_resistances]:- "Added Resistances"
[_metadata_:added_immunities]:- "Added Immunities"
[_metadata_:added_vulnerabilities]:- "Added Vulnerabilities"
[_metadata_:added_conditional_resistances]:- "Added Conditional Resistances"
[_metadata_:added_conditional_immunities]:- "Added Conditional Immunities"
[_metadata_:added_conditional_vulnerabilities]:- "Added Conditional Vulnerabilities"
[_metadata_:charges]:- "Number of charges"
[_metadata_:charge_reset]:- "When do charges reset?"
[_metadata_:has_stat_block]:- "false"

If one item has multiple versions, these are numbered by adding a counter followed by a colon between `_metadata_:` and the specific information, e.g.

[_metadata_:item_name]:- "Item Name"

might become

[_metadata_:1:item_name]:- "Item 1 Name"
[_metadata_:2:item_name]:- "Item 2 Name"
[_metadata_:3:item_name]:- "Item 3 Name"
